Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A spaghetti carbonara with fresh parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Fresh eggs 3
  • Onion
  • Parmesan cheese 300gr
  • bacon strips
  • Rucola
  1. Cook the spaghetti as indicateds on the box.
  2. Cut the onion.
  3. Bake the onion and bacon strips in a pan.
  4. mix 3 fresh eggs with the parmesan cheese. Keep some cheese
  5. Add the bacon and onion to the spaghetti.
  6. Add the egg/parmesan to the pan and stir.
  7. Put it on the plate and finish with the rucola and some parmesan.
  8. Enjoy!>
