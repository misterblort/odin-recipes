Bread with cheese in the microwave

Description

A supersimple yet tasty snack

Ingredients

  • slice of bread
  • Any kind of cheese
  • Spices you like
  1. Put the cheese on the slice of bread
  2. Add spices you like on top of the cheese
  3. Put it in the microwave for 45seconds or untill it is boiling
  4. close the slice of bread and flip it
  5. Let is cool off for a second or 2
  6. Enjoy!
