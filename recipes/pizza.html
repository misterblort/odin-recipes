Pizza

Description

A Pizza with proscuitto and mozarella, finished with basil

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato puree
  • Mozarella
  • Proscuitto
  • Basil
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Spicy oil
  1. Spread the tomato puree on the pizza dough.
  2. Add the proscuitto and mozarella on the pizza.
  3. Put it in the preheated (220deg) oven for 10 minutes.
  4. add the basil and cherry tomatoes for the last 2 minutes.
  5. Finish the dish with some spicy oil.
  6. Enjoy!
